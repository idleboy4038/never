Frequently Asked Questions

What is Never?
Never is a futuristic AI-generated photo app that has the potential and power to turn your selfies into stunning, photorealistic images. It's like having your own personal photographer at your fingertips! With Never, you can create stunning visuals that are guaranteed to make your social media stand out from the crowd. Say goodbye to boring selfies and hello to your best self with Never!
How does the app work?
Never app works like magic! The app uses your selfie to verify your portfolio and generate stunning photo compositions. To create avatars, you need to have NX coins.
What are the key features of the app?
Never app offers a range of exciting features that make it stand out from other photo apps. Some of the key features include AI-generated photorealistic Images, a variety of avatar styles to choose from, easy-to-use interface, and the ability to create stunning visual content for social media. The app also allows users to purchase NX coins to access premium features and generate even more high-quality photos and avatars.
How do I download and install the app?
Downloading and installing Never app is a breeze! Simply go to the App Store or Google Play Store and search for "Never app". Once you find it, click on "Download" or "Install" and the app will automatically start installing on your device. Once it\'s installed, open the app and follow the instructions to start generating your own AI-generated photos!

The app is free to download, but some features require payment. Packages will be available for users to access all features. Keep an eye on our social channels for more information about available packages.
What is the app's privacy policy?
We take user privacy seriously at Never App. Our app is designed to be safe and secure, and we use industry-standard security measures to protect user data. What types of images can the app generate?
Never app can generate a variety of high-quality, AI-generated images, including photorealistic portraits, stylish avatars, and creative compositions up to 4k renders.
Is the app able to generate high-resolution images?
Yes, Never app is able to generate high-resolution images. The app uses advanced AI algorithms to create realistic and detailed images that can be used for various social media platforms.
